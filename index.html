Challenges in Subsurface Detection

Existing GPR systems are expensive, bulky, and difficult to customize, limiting their use. This project bridges the gap by offering:

  • Affordability for institutions and researchers
  • Simple deployment and operation
  • Reliable results in controlled and semi-controlled environments

Engineering Approach

  • Design-first, test-early methodology
  • Hardware-software co-design
  • Emphasis on repeatability and measurement reliability
  • Focus on real-world deployment

How the System Works

  1. Transmit FMCW chirp
  2. Receive reflections from subsurface objects
  3. Mix signals → beat frequency
  4. Digitize using SDR
  5. STFT processing → depth-frequency components
  6. Visualize radargrams
